Who am I?

I’m someone that loves having tons of brain stimulation. Video games, puzzles, or brain teasers. I’ve been told I’m a very sociable person and someone many people can call a friend. I grew up here in St. Louis until the age of 3 when my family and I moved to Portland, Maine. After freezing to death for the next 5 years, we came back here to be closer to family. I’ve lived in St. Louis ever since and mostly enjoyed my stay.
